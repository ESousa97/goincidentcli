## Reporting a Vulnerability

We request that you report vulnerabilities or potential security issues by contacting us via email at: security@example.com.

Please do not open a public issue. We will review the vulnerability and provide an initial response to your disclosure within 48 hours, establishing the next steps and expected remediation timelines.
